Hot Fudge Recipe

This recipe came from my grandmother. I am unsure where it came from, but it is likely to be from a popular cookbook from the 1960s.

Ingredients:

1 1/2 cups sugar

6 tablespoons milk

6 tablespoons butter, salted recommended

1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:

1. Add sugar, milk, and butter to a saucepan* over medium-high heat.

2. Heat to a full* boil, boil for 30 seconds.

3. Take off heat, add chips and stir until fully incorporated.

Notes:

-A heavy-bottomed saucepan is recommended for even cooking and to fully melt the chips into the fudge.

-Take care that it is truly at a gentle but full boil. If you start the 30 seconds while mixture is still at a simmer, it will not reach the proper temperature thus consistency.
