key lime pie

This recipe is from The New Best Recipe Book by Cooks Illustrated.

ingredients:

for the custard:

4 egg yolks*

2 tablespoons lime zest*

1 can condensed milk

1/2 cup key lime juice*

for the crust:

1 cup crushed graham crackers*

5 tablespoons butter, melted, salted recommended

2 tablespoons sugar

directions:

Whisk the yolks with zest until the yolks take on a bit of a green color from the zest, a minute or two.

Add condensed milk and lime juice, let sit for 30 min to thicken. Meanwhile, make the crust.

Combine all ingredients for crust, press into pie pan.

Pour the custard into prepared crust, hit on the counter a bit to pop air bubbles.

Bake at 325Â°F for 17-18 minutes.

notes:

-The higher quality egg, the better.

-Use less if a sweeter pie is desried, a full 2 tablepsoons is quite the punch but very delicious.

-It is not crucial to use key lime specifically, lime will do just fine and people often prefer it as it is a bit less stringent in taste. Also, I recommend using a pre-bottled lime juice such as Nellie & Joe's as it will be more a more consistent flavor than juicing your own limes.

-For the best flavor, use normal graham crackers and crush them yourself; pre-crushed is not as good.
